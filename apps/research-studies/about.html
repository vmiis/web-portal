Download and run this micro web app on a local computer
Clone this micro web app to Glitch and run over there
Steps
  • goto glitch.com (not necessary to have an account)
  • click 'Get Started'-> 'hello-webpage' to create a new app
  • once the app is started, click 'tools'->'import and export'->'Import from GitHub'
  • use this address vmiis/projects:sites/ to import
  • optional: click '.env' to add a 'password_for_file_saving' variable for online editing contents
What are microservices?
Highly maintainable and testable, loosely coupled, independently deployable, organized around business capabilities,owned by a small team, build large business portal by adding microservices together.
