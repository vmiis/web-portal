Sleep

Provides the sleep measures for the night of the user, with the details of each phase of their sleep cycle. Note: if startdate and enddate are separated by more than 24h, enddate will be overwritten with 24h past startdate.

Start Date: Measures' start date.
End Date: Measures' end date.
Data Fields: List of requested data fields, separated by a comma.
hr > Heart Rate. rr > Respiration Rate. snoring > Total snoring time

Sleep summary

Get the basic information about a night of the user, such as the total time they slept, how long it took them to fall asleep, etc.

Start Date: Start date.
End Date: End date.
Data Fields: List of requested data fields, separated by a comma.
nb_rem_episodes > Count of the REM sleep phases. sleep_efficiency > Ratio of the total sleep time over the time spent in bed. sleep_latency > Time spent in bed before falling asleep. total_sleep_time > Total time spent asleep. Sum of light, deep and rem durations. total_timeinbed > Total time spent in bed. wakeup_latency > Time spent in bed after waking up. waso > Time spent awake in bed after falling asleep for the 1st time during the night. asleepduration > Duration of sleep when night comes from external source (light, deep and rem sleep durations are null in this case). breathing_disturbances_intensity > Intensity of breathing disturbances deepsleepduration > Duration in state deep sleep (in seconds). durationtosleep > Time to sleep (in seconds). (deprecated) durationtowakeup > Time to wake up (in seconds). (deprecated) hr_average > Average heart rate. hr_max > Maximal heart rate. hr_min > Minimal heart rate. lightsleepduration > Duration in state light sleep (in seconds). night_events > Events list happened during the night out_of_bed_count > Number of times the user got out of bed during the night. remsleepduration > Duration in state REM sleep (in seconds). rr_average > Average respiration rate. rr_max > Maximal respiration rate. rr_min > Minimal respiration rate. sleep_score > Sleep score snoring > Total snoring time snoringepisodecount > Numbers of snoring episodes of at least one minute wakeupcount > Number of times the user woke up while in bed. Does not include the number of times the user got out of bed. wakeupduration > Time spent awake (in seconds).
